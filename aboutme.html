about me

Introduction

My name is Julia Knoblock ("knob" and "lock" - think of a door) I'm 27 years old and am currently pursuing my interest in front-end web development. Since I was a teenager, I've experimented here and there with coding but never recieved any formal training. Now that I'm learning the "pro" way of doing things, I can't pull myself away. Every little bump I encounter is nearly impossible to ignore until I've overcome it.

Someday I hope to pursure front-end web development as a freelance designer. I've spent years trying to figure out what I would be happy with doing day in and day out, and I think I've found it.

Where I'm From

I'm a rare native San Diegean - born and raised. I say "rare" because more often than not, people come here. There are not many of us who are lucky enough to have been born in such a wonderful laid-back city (and beer capital of the world, I might add). I spent one year living in Los Angeles and quickly decided to come back. There is no leaving San Diego!
